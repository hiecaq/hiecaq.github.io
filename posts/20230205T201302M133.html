Areas of responsibility is a sphere of activity with a standard to be maintained over time(Forte 2017a).

They has a standard of performance to maintain, and has an indefinite end date(Forte 2017a).

They are things we are responible for(Forte 2017b):

  • the roles we take on in life
  • the ongoing standards that maintained by us with efforts
  • things that take a certain amount of constant attention

Usually, any personal relevant information are of Areas.

Area is less actionable(Forte 2017a) than Project. It is usually referred to during weekly review(Forte 2017a).

Reference
