Cornac Ooze Build Report

2025-10-17
Forward links: under:Tales of Maj'Eyal Backward links:
  • under Tales of Maj'Eyal

Winner

Insane Adventure Winner (but 0 death). The build idea is based on (Snarvid 2017), including replies.

This build is too strong for Insane.

Build

Races

Cornac for extra category point.

Stats

in early games, Cunning then Will to get talents unlocked and making Mitosis allowing 4 (which requires 40 CUN). Then will > cun > dex > con.

Use equipments to unlock skin to reduce the need of putting points in con.

Stacking will as much as you can until it reaches like 120. Failing nature power actions is very hard to take into account into the tactics, so I'd rather forget about it.

Prodigies

Adept
+1.5 is super useful, see the subsection
Windtouched Speed
reducing CDs, increasing global speed

Points

  • Call of Ooze at effective 10 is up to 4 ((5/5 + 1.5 with Adept), +0.24 mastery is needed, Ooze heart 0.1 )
  • Poisonous Spore at effective 10 is radius 4 ((5/5 + 1.5 with Adept), +0.24 mastery is needed, Ooze heart 0.1, Sludgegrip 0.2, in this case 3/5 Slime Root and 1/5 Slime Split is enough )

Class Skill Categories

With Adept, All Mucus increases radius by 1 at 2.

The cited guide is still decent although it's written for 1.5.0.
