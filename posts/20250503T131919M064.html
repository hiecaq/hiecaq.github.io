E/N stands for "Everything and Nothing". According to jr: E/N(jr 2017), this term categorizes a type of blog-like personal websites that:

  • Covers a random ranges of topics
  • Has no strong dedicated purpose
  • "Its content means everything to the publisher, but it could mean nothing to the rest of the world".

The difference between E/N and Blog, if there was one, is that a blog is supposed to have a focused purpose.

Reference

jr. 2017. “E/N.” 2017. http://sawv.org/en.html.
