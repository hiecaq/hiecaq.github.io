Builder Lite Pattern

2025-07-19
Forward links: id:Rust related:Design Pattern Backward links:

The Builder Lite Pattern(Kladov 2022) is a variant of the builder pattern that do not have a separated explicit builder class.

It is used in Ratatui(McKinney 2024), for example, due its natural fit to Rust.

Reference

Kladov, Alex. 2022. “Builder Lite.” May 29, 2022. https://matklad.github.io/2022/05/29/builder-lite.html.
McKinney, Josh. 2024. “Builder Lite Pattern.” 2024. https://ratatui.rs/concepts/builder-lite-pattern/.
