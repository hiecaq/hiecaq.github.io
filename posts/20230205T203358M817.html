Archives is "inactive items from the other 3 categories(Forte 2017)"

Examples:

  • projects that have been completed or become inactive
  • areas that we are no longer committed to maintaining
  • resources that we are no longer interested in

Archive is least actionable(Forte 2017).

They are portfolio of completed projects(Forte 2017):

  • inactive
  • ready to offer up potentially useful material to reuse and recycle in future projects
  • easier to find assets based on when it was created and which project it was associated with
  • usable for project retrospectives, annual retrospectives, and resume and proposals.

Reference

Forte, Tiago. 2017. “The Para Method: A Universal System for Organizing Digital Information.” The P.A.R.A. Method. February 8, 2017. https://fortelabs.com/blog/para/.
